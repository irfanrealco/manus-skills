# Vertical Scaling Playbook

## The Core Principle

Vertical scaling means taking one proven product architecture and re-skinning it for a new industry. The transformation pattern stays the same. The language, pain points, price ceiling, and community channels change.

The fastest way to go vertical: copy the product architecture doc, change the industry-specific language in Layers 1 and 2, re-run the market signal scan for the new vertical, and rebuild the landing page with new copy.

---

## Vertical Templates

### Legal

**Target buyer:** Solo attorneys, small law firms (2–10 attorneys)
**Core transformation:** "A solo attorney who spends 3+ hours per week on research and document drafting becomes someone with an AI research assistant that handles first drafts in minutes."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $799 (attorneys bill $300–$500/hr; 2 hours of their time = $600–$1,000)
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $3,999
**Top communities:** r/Lawyertalk, r/LegalAdvice (for research), ABA communities, state bar association forums
**Key pain points:** Document drafting time, research overhead, client intake processing, billing reconciliation
**Compliance note:** Always clarify that the AI does not provide legal advice — it assists the attorney.

---

### Healthcare (Non-Clinical)

**Target buyer:** Private practice administrators, medical office managers, health coaches
**Core transformation:** "A health coach who manually writes every client progress note becomes someone with an AI that generates structured notes from voice recordings in under 2 minutes."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $599
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $2,499
**Top communities:** r/HealthcareIT, r/MedicalPracticeManagement, health coach Facebook groups
**Key pain points:** Documentation time, appointment scheduling, patient follow-up, billing codes
**Compliance note:** HIPAA applies. Do not store patient data. Clarify data handling upfront.

---

### Real Estate

**Target buyer:** Independent agents, small brokerages (1–5 agents)
**Core transformation:** "A real estate agent who spends 2 hours per listing on descriptions, emails, and follow-ups becomes someone with an AI that handles all written communication in 15 minutes."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $499
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $1,999
**Top communities:** r/realestate, r/RealEstateAgent, BiggerPockets, local agent Facebook groups
**Key pain points:** Listing description writing, lead follow-up emails, market report generation, client communication
**Design note:** Real estate buyers respond to social proof from named agents in specific markets. Use location-specific testimonials.

---

### E-Commerce

**Target buyer:** Shopify/WooCommerce store owners doing $10K–$500K/year
**Core transformation:** "An e-commerce operator who manually writes product descriptions, responds to customer emails, and builds reports becomes someone with an AI stack that handles all three automatically."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $699
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $2,999
**Top communities:** r/ecommerce, r/shopify, Shopify Community forums, e-commerce Facebook groups
**Key pain points:** Product description writing, customer service volume, inventory reporting, ad copy generation
**Integration note:** Shopify API access is usually required. Confirm before scoping.

---

### Coaching & Consulting

**Target buyer:** Business coaches, life coaches, consultants with 5–20 active clients
**Core transformation:** "A coach who spends 4+ hours per week on session prep, notes, and follow-up becomes someone with an AI that handles all three, letting them focus on the actual coaching."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $599
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $2,499
**Top communities:** r/Entrepreneur, r/smallbusiness, coach-specific Facebook groups, LinkedIn
**Key pain points:** Session note-taking, client progress tracking, proposal writing, content creation
**Positioning note:** Coaches are highly skeptical of AI replacing human connection. Position as "handles the admin so you can be more present."

---

### SaaS / Developer Tools

**Target buyer:** Solo SaaS founders, developer tool builders, indie hackers
**Core transformation:** "A solo SaaS founder who manually handles support tickets, writes documentation, and monitors errors becomes someone with an AI stack that handles all three, letting them ship features instead."
**Entry tier price ceiling:** $799
**Expansion tier price ceiling:** $3,499
**Top communities:** r/SaaS, r/indiehackers, Indie Hackers community, Hacker News, Product Hunt
**Key pain points:** Support ticket volume, documentation maintenance, error monitoring, onboarding email sequences
**Technical note:** This vertical expects API access and integration capability. Scope carefully before pricing.

## Vertical Adaptation Checklist

When adapting to a new vertical, update these elements:

- [ ] Target buyer persona (name, context, emotional drivers, objections)
- [ ] Core transformation statement (before/after language)
- [ ] Pain points in the problem section (use real quotes from the vertical's communities)
- [ ] Price (re-run Pricing Triangle for the new vertical's buyer)
- [ ] Community channels for Week 3 marketing activation
- [ ] Testimonials (source from the new vertical's communities)
- [ ] Compliance notes (legal, healthcare, financial verticals have specific requirements)
- [ ] Landing page hero image (generate a new one specific to the vertical's aesthetic)

Elements that stay the same across verticals:
- Product architecture (3-layer model)
- Two-tier structure
- Build plan timeline
- AI model routing rule
- Design system tokens (unless the vertical demands a different aesthetic)
