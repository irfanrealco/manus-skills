# Marketing Brief Template

## Document Header

```
# Marketing Brief: [Product Name]
**Document Type:** Internal Marketing Brief
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** [date]
**Status:** Ready for Review
```

## Required Sections (in order)

### 1. Executive Summary (1 paragraph)
Describe the product, its price, its target buyer, and the design/brand positioning. This paragraph should be readable in 30 seconds and convey the full picture.

### 2. Product Overview
Two subsections:
- **The Two-Tier Offering**: Describe each tier with name, price, delivery mechanism, and what the buyer gets. Explain why the two-tier structure exists.
- **The Market Opportunity**: Describe the gap in the market, documented demand signals, and why now is the right time.

### 3. Target Audience
Two fully profiled personas:
- **Primary Persona**: Name/archetype, job/context, emotional drivers (what they want to feel), key objections (what stops them from buying)
- **Secondary Persona**: Same structure, focused on the expansion tier buyer

### 4. Messaging Hierarchy
A 7-row table mapping each message level to its content and placement:

| Level | Message | Where It Appears |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | [transformation statement] | Hero headline |
| Secondary | [mechanism + trust signal] | Hero subheadline |
| Proof | [social proof number] | Trust bar |
| Differentiator | [what makes this different] | Problem section |
| Value anchor | [price statement] | Pricing section |
| Expansion | [next product teaser] | Expansion section |
| Trust close | [urgency/availability signal] | Below CTA |

### 5. The Pricing Card
Describe the component: what it does visually, how the animations work, the conversion principles it is designed around, and how it aligns with the design system.

### 6. Campaign Channels & Launch Sequence
Three phases:
- **Week 1 — Organic Seeding**: A table of specific communities, subreddits, or channels with the framing for each post
- **Week 2–4 — Content Proof**: What content to create from real client sessions
- **Month 2 — Paid Amplification**: Only after 3 paid clients. Specific ad creative description and budget.

### 7. Success Metrics
A table with 30-day and 90-day targets for: unique visitors, CTA click rate, paid clients (by tier), revenue, testimonials collected, and avg. session-to-close time.

### 8. Immediate Action Items
Exactly 3 actions, numbered, in priority order. Each action should be specific enough to complete in under 2 hours.
